Mr Johnson

We are Mr Johnson! Forged and refined in the heart of England. We play music, we sing songs and we have fun doing it. Mr Johnson play all original songs (except one very obscure cover), that range from energetic folk-rock songs about the world we live in, to chilled, lounge-esque tracks that touch on the subject of dreams and love.

About Mr Johnson

Lee is the lead singer, guitarist, and has written almost all of the songs that Mr Johnson perform. Lee writes songs that cover politics, heartbreak, love, loss and also there's a song about a dog in there somewhere. When asked about his influences, he looked wistfully into the distance and simply said "Life... also Pinegrove".

Neil, flute player and backing vocals (and occasional lead vocals). The flute is a strange instrument, which is okay because he plays it in a strange way. Musical influences include Jethro Tull, Broken Family Band, Turin Brakes, and depressing songs in general. He is a really cheery guy overall, he even told a joke once.

Hello, everyone. This is Gary, he plays drums for Mr Johnson. He likes strong tea and chocolate digestives... and astrophysics. His musical influences range from Led Zeppelin, through to Gomez, ending up in The Stone Roses with a slight detour to James Brown.

Jay plays the bass. He said that was already too much personal information for the world to know. Jay prefers to just stand at the back and do his thing, which usually playing bass if he hasn't been distracted by something shiny. His playing style is heavily influenced by Peter Hook of Joy Divison and Carlos Dengler, formerly of Interpol.
